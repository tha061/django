If you see your own funeral in a dream, it may indicate that you are giving up part of yourself or an old way of acting. The direct meaning of this dream symbolizes your ignore about the important situation and your unwillingness to solve it.
If you see a dream in which you are at somebody else's funeral may indicate that you're burying the old relationship and close the lid of the past. Most likely, you let go of the old feelings and emotions that have been stored inside of you. Your death in a dream can give you courage and confidence for the next step to your independence from your parents or from a person close to you.
