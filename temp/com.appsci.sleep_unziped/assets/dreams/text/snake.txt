The dream in which you see a snake symbolizes feminine energy, healing and spirituality. If you had a dream in which a snake bit you, then you should not worry too much as this dream has a positive meaning. The dream means that you accept all parts of your nature, becoming a stronger person. If you see a dream in which you are trying to fight, attack or kill a snake, it means that in real life you will have to protect your reputation from unpleasant gossip. If the attempt to kill a snake in a dream was successful, you will succeed against all hostilities.
