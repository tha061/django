   Blog    Help    Download    ☰  × ProBikeGarage Privacy Policy  This Privacy Policy tells you what to expect ProBikeGarage to do with your personal information when you contact us or use our Services.  The www.probikegarage.com owners (“ProBikeGarage”) are the controllers for the personal information we process unless otherwise stated. You can contact us by sending an email to contact@probikegarage.com .  What personal data do we collect?  Strava access token and activities  By connecting to the Services using Strava  you grant ProBikeGarage access to your public and private Strava activities and personal data. In order for us to provide you with our Services  we need to store a Strava API access token so we can make some read operations on your behalf without the need to know your password.  We also store all your Strava bike activities (rides  virtual rides  e-bike rides  etc.) to provide fast access to your data.  Your name and email  We need your name and email to personalize the Services and send you related communications. These could go from notifications needed for the Services to app update announcements. You can opt-out non-critical communications.  We collect this information from the 3rd-party sites that you can use for authentication (e.g. Strava).  Device information  In order to send you notifications required by the Services to your devices  we need to store a list of unique identifiers that represent your devices.  We collect this information after you create an account or login into an existing account in a new device.  Anonymous usage data  We collect some anonymous data related to the Services usage and errors to maintain and improve the Services.  Why we collect personal data?  ProBikeGarage relies on the following legal bases to collect and process your personal information:    Necessary to provide the Services as defined in the Terms of Service .    Necessary to pursue the legitimate interests of ProBikeGarage and its users in ensuring the Services correct operation.    How we protect information?  We take several and up-to-date measures to safeguard the collection  transmission  and storage of the data we collect.  While ProBikeGarage continues to work hard to protect your personal information  no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be absolutely secure  and ProBikeGarage cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to ProBikeGarage. Transmitting personal information is done at your own risk.  How we share information?  We do not share any of your personal information with any 3rd-party unless required by ProBikeGarage to provide you with our Services. Those 3rd-parties include email providers and device notifications providers.  How long we store information?  We store your information as long as it is necessary to provide the Services to you and others. Information associated with your account will generally be kept until it is no longer necessary to provide the Services or until you ask us to delete it or your account is deleted  whichever comes first.  What are your rights?  The following rights are applicable to all EU members based on the GDPR  but we extend them to all of our users Worldwide.  Access and Porting  You can access much of your information by logging into the Services with your account. If you require additional access  contact us at support@probikegarage.com .  Rectify  Restrict  Limit  Delete  You can also rectify  restrict  limit or delete much of your information by logging into the Services. If you are unable to do this or require an additional action  contact us at support@probikegarage.com .  Object  Where we process your information based on our legitimate interests explained above you can object to this processing in certain circumstances. In such cases  we will cease processing your information unless we have compelling legitimate grounds to continue processing or where it is needed for legal reasons.  Complain  Whether you wish to raise a concern about our use of your information (and without prejudice to any other rights you may have)  you have the right to do so with your local supervisory authority.  Modifications  ProBikeGarage reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. ProBikeGarage will notify you of any significant changes to the Privacy Policy. Join the community!
